• But Baldad the Suhite answering, said: Power and terror is with him, that maketh concord in his high ones. Is there any number of his soldiers? And upon whom shall not his light arise? Can man be justified compared with God, or the born of a woman appear clean? Behold the moon also doth not shine, and the stars are not clean in his sight. How much more man rottenness, and the son of man a worm?
