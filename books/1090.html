• AND there came to him the Pharisees and Sadducees tempting: and they demanded him to shew them a sign from Heaven. But he answered & said to them: When it is evening, you say: It will be fair-weather, for the element is red. And in the morning: This day there will be a tempest, for the element doth glow and lower. The face therefore of the element you have skill to discern: & the signs of times can you not? The naughty and adulterous Generation seeketh for a sign: and there shall not a sign be given it, but the sign of Jonas the Prophet. And he left them and went away.
