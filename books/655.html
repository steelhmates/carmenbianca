• Of understanding to David, when he was in the cave, a prayer. With my voice I have cried to our Lord: with my voice I have prayed to our Lord: I pour out my prayer in his sight, and I pronounce my tribulation before him. When my spirit faileth of myself, and thou hast known my paths. In this way, which I walked, they hid a snare for me. I looked toward the right hand, and saw: and there was none that would know me. Flight hath failed me: and there is none to require my soul. I have cried to thee, o Lord, I have said:
