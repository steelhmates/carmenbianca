And Jonathas came to meet the king into Joppe with glory, and they saluted one another, and they slept there. And Jonathas went with the king even to the river, that was called Eleutherus, and he returned into Jerusalem. And Demetrius the king seeing that the land was quiet in his sight, & nothing resisted him, he dismissed all his army, every man to his place, except the foreign army, which he brought from the isles of the Gentiles: and all the bands of his fathers were enemies to him.
