• AND entering in, he walked through Jericho. And behold a man named Zachaeus: and this was a Prince of the Publicans, and he rich. And he sought to see JESUS what he was, and he could not for the multitude, because he was little of stature. And running before, he went up into a sycamore tree that he might see him: because he was to pass by it. And when he was come to the place, JESUS looking up, saw him, and said to him: Zachaeus, come down in haste: because this day I must abide in thy house. And he in haste came down, and received him rejoicing. And when all saw it, they murmured saying, that he turned in, to a man that was a sinner. But Zachaeus standing, said to our Lord: Behold the half of my goods, Lord, I give to the poor: and if I have defrauded any man of any thing, I restore fourfold. JESUS said to him: That this day salvation is made to this house: because that he also is the son of Abraham. For the Son of man is come to seek and to save that which was lost. They hearing these things, he added and spake a parable, for that he was nigh to Jerusalem, and because they thought that forthwith the Kingdom of God should be manifested. He said therefore: A certain noble man went into a far country to take to himself a kingdom, and to return.
