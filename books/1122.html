• AND JESUS full of the Holy Ghost, returned from Jordan, & was driven in the spirit into the desert. forty days, and was tempted of the devil. And he did eat nothing in those days; and when they were ended, he was an hungered. And the devil said to him: If thou be the Son of God, say to this stone that it be made bread. And JESUS made answer unto him: It is written, That not in bread alone shall man live, but in every word of God. And the devil brought him into an high mountain, and shewed him all the kingdoms of the whole world in a moment of time; and he said to him: To thee will I give this whole power, and the glory of them; for to me they are delivered, and to whom I will, I do give them. Thou therefore if thou wilt adore before me, they shall all be thine. And JESUS answering said to him: It is written, Thou shalt adore the Lord thy God & him only shalt thou serve. And he brought him into Jerusalem, & set him upon the pinnacle of the Temple; and he said to him: If thou be the Son of God, cast thyself from hence downward. For it is written, that He hath given his Angels charge of thee, that they preserve thee: and that in their hands they shall bear thee up, lest perhaps thou knock thy foot against a stone. And JESUS answering said to him: It is said, Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy God. And all the tentation being ended, the devil departed from him until a time. And JESUS returned in the force of the spirit into Galilee, and the fame went forth through the whole country of him. And he taught in their Synagogues, and was magnified of all. And he came to Nazareth where he was brought up: and he entered according to his custom on the Sabboth day into the Synagogue; and he rose up to read. And the book of Esay the Prophet was delivered unto him. And as he unfolded the book, he found the place where it was written: The Spirit of the Lord upon me, for which he anointed me, to evangelize unto the poor he sent me, to heal the contrite of heart, to preach to the captives remission, and sight to the blind, to dismiss the bruised unto remission, to preach the acceptable year of the Lord, and the day of retribution. And when he had folded the book, he rendered it to the minister, and sat down. And the eyes of all in the Synagogue were bent upon him. And he began to say unto them: That this day is fulfilled this scripture in your ears.
