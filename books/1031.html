In the first chariot red horses, and in the second chariot black horses, and in the third chariot white horses, and in the fourth chariot speckled horses, and strong. And I answered, and said to the Angel, that spake in me: What are these things, my Lord? And the Angel answered, and said to me: These are the four winds of heaven, which go forth to stand before the Dominatour of all the earth. That, in which were the black horses went forth into the land of the North, and the white went forth after them: and the speckled went forth to the land of the South.
