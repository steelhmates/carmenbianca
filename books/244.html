• And it came to pass when Samuel waxed old, he appointed his sons judges over Israel. And the name of his first begotten son was Joel: and the name of the second Abia, judges in Bersabee. And his sons walked not in his ways: but they declined after avarice, & took bribes, and perverted judgment. Therefore all the ancients of Israel being assembled, came to Samuel into Ramatha. And they said to him: Behold thou art old, and thy sons walk not in thy ways: appoint us a king, that he may judge us, as also all nations have. Now therefore hear their voice: but yet testify to them, and foretell them the right of the king, that shall reign over them.
