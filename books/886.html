• The word that was made to Jeremy from our Lord in the days of Joakim the son of Josias the king of Juda, saying: Go to the house of the Rechabites: and speak to them, & thou shalt bring them into the house of our Lord, into one chamber of the treasuries, & thou shalt give them wine to drink. And I took Jezonias the son of Jeremias the son of Habsamias, and his brethren, and all his sons, and the whole house of the Rechabites. And I brought them into the house of our Lord, to the treasure-house of the sons of Hanan, the son of Jegedelias the man of God, which was by the treasure-house of the Princes, above the treasure of Maasias the son of Sellum, who was keeper of the entry. And I set before the sons of the house of the Rechabites goblets full of wine, and cups: and I said to them: Drink ye wine. Who answered: We will not drink wine: because Jonadab the son of Rechab, our father, commanded us, saying:
