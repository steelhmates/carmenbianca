  • We will magnify our tongue, our lips are of us, who is our Lord? For the misery of the needy, and mourning of the poor, now will I arise, saith our Lord: I will put in a salvation, I will do confidently in him. Words of our Lord be chaste words: silver examined by fire, tried from the earth, purged seven fold. Thou Lord wilt preserve us: and keep us from this generation for ever. The impious walk round about: according to thy highness thou hast multiplied the children of men.
