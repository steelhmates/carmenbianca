And Nathan said to the king: All that is in thy heart, go do, because our Lord is with thee. And it came to pass in that night: and behold the word of our Lord to Nathan, saying: Go, and speak to my servant David: Thus saith our Lord: Shalt thou build me a house to dwell in? For neither have I dwelt in house from the day that I brought the children of Israel out of the Land of Aegypt, until this day: but I walked in tabernacle, and in tent.
