  • Woe is me wretch, because our Lord hath added sorrow to my sorrow: I have laboured in my mourning, & have not found rest. Thus saith our Lord: Thus shalt thou say to him: Behold, them whom I have builded, I do destroy: and whom I have planted, I do pluck up, and all this land. And doest thou seek to thyself great things? Seek not: for behold I will bring evil upon all flesh, saith our Lord: and I will give thee thy life into safety in all places, whither-soever thou shalt go.
