• A Psalm to Asaph. God stood in the assembly of gods, and in the midst he judgeth gods. How long judge ye iniquity: and accept ye the persons of sinners. Judge ye for the needy and the pupil: justify ye the humble, and the poor. Take away the poor: and deliver the needy from the hand of the sinner. They knew not, neither did they understand, they walk in darkness: all the foundations of the earth shall be moved. I said:
