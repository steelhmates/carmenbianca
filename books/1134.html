And the bailiff said within himself: What shall I do, because my lord taketh away from me the bailiffship? Dig I am not able, to beg I am ashamed. I know what I will do, that when I shall be removed from the bailiffship, they may receive me into their houses. And there was a certain beggar called Lazarus, that lay at his gate, full of sores: desiring to be filled of the crumbs, that fell from the rich man's table, but the dogs also came, and licked his sores. And it came to pass that the beggar died, and was carried of the Angels into Abraham's bosom. And the rich man also died: and he was buried in hell.
