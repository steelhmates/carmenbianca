He, when he had seen Peter and John about to enter into the temple, asked to receive an alms. But Peter with John looking upon him, said:

And taking his right hand, he lifted him up, and forthwith his feet & soles were made strong. And springing he stood, & walked; & went in with them into the temple walking, and leaping, and praising God. And all the people saw him walking and praising God. And they knew him, that it was he which sat for alms at the Specious gate of the temple: and they were exceedingly astonished and aghast at that that had chanced to him. And as he held Peter and John, all the people ran to them unto the porch which is called Salomon's, wondering. But Peter seeing them, made answer to the people: Ye men of Israel, why marvel you at this, or why look you upon us, as though by our power or holiness we have made this man to walk? The God of Abraham, and the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob, the God of our fathers hath glorified his Son JESUS, whom you indeed delivered & denied before the face of Pilate, he judging him to be released. But you denied the Holy and the Just One, and asked a man-killer to be given unto you. But the Author of life you killed, whom God hath raised from the dead, of which we are witnesses. And in the faith of his name, this man whom you see and know, his name hath strengthened; & the faith which is by him, hath given this perfect health in the sight of all you. And now (brethren) I know that you did it through ignorance, as also your princes. But God who foreshewed by the mouth of all the Prophets that his CHRIST should suffer, hath so fulfilled it. Be Penitent therefore & convert, that your sins may be put out. That, when the times shall come of refreshing by the sight of our Lord, and he shall send him that hath been preached unto you JESUS CHRIST.
